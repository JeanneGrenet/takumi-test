# Understanding Content Collections

Content collections in Astro provide a type-safe way to manage your content.

## Key Features

1. **Type Safety** - Full TypeScript support for your frontmatter
2. **Validation** - Zod schemas ensure data consistency
3. **Flexibility** - Support for Markdown, MDX, and more

This makes managing blog posts, documentation, and other content a breeze!
