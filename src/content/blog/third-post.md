# Building Dynamic OG Images

Dynamic OG images can significantly improve your content's appearance when shared on social media.

## Benefits

- **Consistent Branding** - Match your site's design
- **Dynamic Content** - Include post titles and metadata
- **Better Engagement** - Stand out in social feeds

Using libraries like `@takumi-rs/image-response`, you can generate these images on-the-fly!
